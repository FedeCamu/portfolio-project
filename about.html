Hi, I am Federica C.

Front-end Developer, based in Ontario Canada

As a web developer with a background in architecture, I bring over 20 years of design and problem-solving experience to create websites that don’t just look great but work seamlessly. My goal is to partner with you to bring your vision to life—whether it’s crafting a responsive, user-friendly design or ensuring your site is optimized to reach the right audience.

When I transitioned into the tech world in 2022, I dove into coding through SheCodes workshops, where I mastered web development essentials like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.
My architectural experience taught me how to blend form and function, which now allows me to design websites that are not only visually engaging but also intuitive and efficient for your users.

I specialize in building responsive, SEO-optimized websites that work beautifully on both desktop and mobile devices. From creating polished designs with frameworks like Bootstrap to adding dynamic functionality with APIs, I’m committed to delivering solutions that meet your goals and exceed your expectations.

More than just a developer, I see myself as your partner—someone who listens to your needs, understands your challenges, and works with you to build a website that truly represents your brand and connects with your audience.
